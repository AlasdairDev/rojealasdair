Founded in 2024 as a DIY independent label in Manila, built by someone who understands the highs and lows of self-releasing. Rooted in underground music, built around creative autonomy.

We empower artists to retain full creative control. Every release is shaped through genuine collaboration. The final product always reflects the artist's true vision, without compromise.

More than a platform, we're a community. Through distribution, marketing, and a growing network, we help unique voices reach wider audiences while staying true to their roots.
